What do you mean, 'open meaning'?

Meaning is one of the most fundamental human faculties. We have it and use it without noticing it. It influences our thoughts, actions and interactions. But what happens when that fundamental faculty is given to machines?

Open vector spaces: what does the machine mean?

We are developing web applications that let you play with machine-constructed meaning, so that you get a better feel of how things work, and can investigate potential problems, such as biases in the data.

For developers: free vectors and code!

We are working on providing meaning representations that are open and free, directly usable in your applications. Download our spaces or just a few vectors from our API. All code used on this site is open source.
