Hey there! You have come to our prototype for visualising areas of so-called semantic spaces. It shows you what a computer who's read Wikipedia in a particular language can learn about the meaning of words in that language.

Currently, the demo is in Catalan. You can type a word (e.g. casa, house) and see which other words are related to it.
